# Professional Experience & Project Highlights — Nimbus

### Role and Duration

* **Company:** Nimbus (Consumer fintech startup)
* **Position:** Product Manager — owned the core budgeting and subscription product from 0 to $50M ARR
* **Duration:** January 2021 to Present (4 years)

### Key Work Areas and Achievements

#### 1. Activation & Onboarding

Led redesign of the new-user onboarding flow to reduce time-to-value.

* **Onboarding Funnel Overhaul:** Redesigned 6-step onboarding to 3-step by eliminating low-signal questions; ran A/B test across 40K users.
  * Increased Day-7 activation rate by **35%**.
  * Reduced onboarding drop-off from 62% to 41%.
* **Bank Connection Reliability:** Partnered with engineering to replace the legacy Plaid integration with a dual-provider fallback.
  * Improved bank-link success rate from 71% to **89%**, directly unblocking the activation loop.

#### 2. Monetization & Subscription Growth

Owned the full subscription funnel from trial to paid conversion.

* **Paywall Redesign:** Redesigned the premium upgrade paywall with social proof and usage-based nudges; tested 4 variants.
  * Lifted trial-to-paid conversion by **22%**, contributing **$2.1M incremental ARR**.
* **Annual Plan Push:** Introduced annual plan as default with monthly downgrade option.
  * Increased annual plan mix from 34% to **58%**, improving LTV by 1.7x.
* **Churn Intervention Flow:** Built a cancellation-intent save flow with targeted offers based on usage segment.
  * Reduced voluntary churn by **18%** in the 90 days post-launch.

#### 3. Engagement & Notifications

Built the behavioral notifications system to drive weekly active usage.

* **Smart Notification Engine:** Designed a rules-based notification system using spend triggers and budget threshold signals.
  * Increased 30-day notification opt-in rate to **74%** (up from 51%).
  * Weekly active users grew **29%** in the 60 days after rollout.
* **Personalized Budget Insights:** Shipped a weekly spend summary card with category-level anomaly detection.
  * Feature became the top-rated in the app store within 3 months (4.7 stars, **2,400+ reviews** mentioning "insights").

#### 4. Platform & Data Infrastructure

Collaborated with data and engineering to build the analytics foundation.

* **Event Taxonomy:** Defined and standardized 120+ product events across mobile and web using Amplitude.
  * Enabled the first cross-platform funnel reports, reducing analysis turnaround from 5 days to **same day**.
* **Experimentation Framework:** Worked with engineering to build an internal A/B testing framework.
  * Enabled the team to run **3x more experiments per quarter** without additional engineering overhead.
