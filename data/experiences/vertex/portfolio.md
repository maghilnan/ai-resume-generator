# Professional Experience & Project Highlights — Vertex Analytics

### Role and Duration

* **Company:** Vertex Analytics (B2B analytics consulting)
* **Position:** Business Analyst — data and product analyst for retail and CPG client engagements
* **Duration:** August 2017 to June 2019 (2 years)

### Key Work Areas and Achievements

#### 1. Store Performance and Location Optimization

Led development of a location analytics tool for retail clients to optimize distribution and new store planning.

* **Site Selection Tool:** Built a map-based analytical application enabling market managers to identify optimal locations for new store openings.
  * Drove a **14% lift in average store sales** for locations opened using the tool's recommendations.
  * Modeled cannibalization impact for a pipeline of **150 planned new stores** to inform market expansion strategy.
* **Store Productivity Modeling:** Built regression models identifying key drivers of store productivity (demographics, hyperlocal data, execution KPIs).
  * Enabled prediction of incremental revenue opportunities at the store level.
* **Risk Analysis:** Analyzed performance data from closed locations to surface failure drivers, providing actionable recommendations to prevent future closures.

#### 2. Customer Analytics and Churn

Applied data mining techniques to improve customer experience and retention outcomes for a telecommunications client.

* **Interaction Analytics:** Analyzed millions of customer care call transcripts using text mining to identify top call drivers and post-call behavior patterns.
  * Recommended IVR menu restructuring that **reduced customer wait times by 25%**.
* **Churn Prediction:** Used association learning to identify behavioral patterns predictive of customer churn.
  * Findings informed a proactive retention program targeting at-risk segments.

#### 3. Automation and Leadership

* **Reporting Automation:** Engineered automated pipelines for store performance and traffic reporting.
  * Reduced manual reporting turnaround time by **60%**.
* **Mentoring:** Managed daily deliverables for junior analysts over 8 months and served as an internal Python instructor, training **80+ new hires** in data fundamentals.
