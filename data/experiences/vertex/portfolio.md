# Professional Experience & Project Highlights — Vertex Analytics

### Role and Duration

* **Company:** Vertex Analytics (B2B analytics consulting, boutique firm)
* **Position:** Business Analyst — led data and product analytics engagements for retail, CPG, and telecommunications clients
* **Duration:** August 2017 to June 2019 (2 years)
* **Clients:** Fortune 500 telecom, national specialty retailer, regional CPG brand, regional grocery chain

---

### Key Work Areas and Achievements

#### 1. Retail Store Performance and Location Optimization

Led end-to-end development of a location intelligence and store performance platform for a national specialty retailer.

* **Site Selection Tool:** Built a map-based analytical web application enabling regional market managers to identify optimal locations for new store openings, drawing on demographic, competitive, and real estate data.
  * Drove a **14% lift in average store sales** for locations opened using the tool's recommendations vs. pre-tool openings.
  * Modeled cannibalization impact for a pipeline of **150 planned new stores** across 12 markets.
* **Trade Area Definition:** Developed customer-journey-based trade area models using credit card transaction data and mobile location signals to replace legacy radius-based definitions.
  * Improved trade area accuracy (overlap with actual customer origin) from 58% to **82%**.
* **Store Productivity Regression Model:** Built OLS and gradient-boosted regression models identifying key drivers of store-level productivity — demographics (income, age, household size), hyperlocal characteristics (proximity to anchors, traffic counts), and execution KPIs (planogram compliance, staff tenure).
  * Model explained **74% of variance** in same-store sales; used by the real estate team for underwriting new leases.
* **Closure Risk Scoring:** Analyzed 3 years of performance data from 40+ closed locations to identify leading indicators of failure (declining basket size, traffic decline rate, demographic shift index).
  * Produced a quarterly closure risk score for all 600+ stores; flagged 11 stores that were subsequently approved for remediation programs.
* **Revenue Optimization Deep-Dive:** Decomposed store revenue into traffic, conversion, basket size, and return rate components; identified cross-sell and upsell gaps in 3 store clusters.
  * Findings directly informed a targeted employee training program; pilot stores saw a **9% improvement in basket size** over 2 quarters.

#### 2. Telecom Customer Analytics and Churn

Applied advanced data mining to improve customer experience and reduce churn for a Fortune 500 telecommunications client.

* **Customer Care Interaction Analytics:** Analyzed 4M+ customer care voice call transcripts using NLP and text mining to identify primary call drivers, sentiment patterns, and post-call behavior (repeat calls, churn within 30 days).
  * Built a call driver taxonomy with 22 categories adopted by the client's CX team as the operational standard.
  * Identified that "billing confusion" (28% of contacts) and "feature discovery" (19%) were the top addressable drivers.
* **IVR Optimization:** Used call driver analysis to recommend a restructured IVR menu that front-loaded the top 3 call reasons.
  * Recommended restructuring **reduced average customer wait time by 25%** (from 4.2 min to 3.1 min) in A/B test.
  * Self-serve IVR resolution rate improved from 31% to **44%**, reducing live-agent contact volume.
* **Churn Prediction Model:** Built a binary churn prediction model using association learning and logistic regression on 18 months of behavioral data (usage patterns, billing events, call frequency, plan changes).
  * Model achieved **AUC of 0.83** on holdout set; deployed as a weekly batch score for the retention team.
  * Retention team using the model's top-decile scores outperformed the control group by **3.2x** in save rate over 90 days.
* **High-Value Customer Segmentation:** Segmented the customer base into 6 behavioral clusters using k-means on usage, tenure, and ARPU data; profiled each segment for churn risk and upsell propensity.
  * Enabled targeted intervention programs for the two highest-risk segments; estimated **$3.1M in prevented churn revenue** over 12 months.
* **Net Promoter Score Driver Analysis:** Ran regression on post-interaction NPS data to identify the top predictors of detractor scores.
  * Found first-call resolution rate was the single strongest predictor; led to a coaching program for agents with low FCR that lifted overall NPS by **+8 points** over 2 quarters.

#### 3. CPG Shelf and Pricing Analytics

Conducted shelf optimization and pricing elasticity analysis for a regional CPG brand.

* **Shelf Space ROI Analysis:** Analyzed sales velocity by shelf position (eye level, mid, low) and facings count across 200+ retail partners.
  * Found that increasing facings from 2 to 3 for the core SKU drove a **17% sales lift** on average; provided a data brief used in buyer negotiations.
* **Pricing Elasticity Modeling:** Built price elasticity models for the top 8 SKUs using historical price promotion and scan data.
  * Identified 3 SKUs that were being promoted below their optimal discount depth; shifting to the modeled optimal depth improved **promotional ROI by 23%**.
* **New Product Launch Tracker:** Built a real-time distribution and velocity tracker for a new product line launch across 4 retail banners.
  * Flagged distribution gaps in 2 regions within 2 weeks of launch; client's sales team resolved gaps 3 weeks faster than prior launches.
* **Competitive Price Monitoring:** Set up a weekly competitive price scraping and alerting pipeline covering 12 competitor SKUs across 5 retail chains.
  * Gave the client's pricing team 48-hour advance notice of competitive price moves, enabling proactive response instead of reactive.

#### 4. Grocery Loyalty and Basket Analysis

Led a basket and loyalty analytics engagement for a regional grocery chain.

* **Loyalty Segmentation:** Segmented 1.2M loyalty card holders into 5 tiers using recency, frequency, and monetary (RFM) analysis.
  * Top 20% of members drove **61% of total revenue**; findings informed a tiered rewards program redesign.
* **Basket Affinity Analysis:** Used market basket analysis (Apriori algorithm) on 18 months of transaction data to identify high-affinity product pairs and category cross-sell opportunities.
  * Produced a planogram recommendation for 3 high-traffic aisles; test stores implementing the recommendation saw a **7% lift in basket size**.
* **Churn Early Warning:** Built a 30-day lapse prediction model for loyalty members (no purchase in 30+ days) using visit frequency decay and last-visit basket size.
  * Re-engagement email campaign targeting the model's top decile achieved a **19% win-back rate** vs. 6% for the control group.
